# 🖐️ Gesture Mouse v3.0 — The Ultimate Cheat Sheet

Welcome to your completely hands-free virtual mouse! The system relies on tracking two distinct hands. Your **Right Hand** generally acts as your physical mouse, while your **Left Hand** acts as your keyboard and system control.

---

## 🖱️ RIGHT HAND — Mouse Control
The right hand focuses strictly on navigating your computer and clicking. Keep your hand facing the camera (palm forward or slightly tilted). 

| Hand Pose | What it looks like | Action Performed |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Index Up** | ☝️ Only index finger pointing up | **Moves the cursor** across the screen. |
| **Brief Pinch** | 🤌 Tap your thumb and index together | **Left Click** (Standard click). |
| **Middle Up** | 🖕 Only the middle finger up | **Right Click** (Opens context menus). |
| **Two Fingers Up**| ✌️ Index and middle fingers "V" sign | **Scroll Mode** (Move your hand up/down to scroll a page). |
| **Pinch & Hold** | 🤌 Touch thumb and index, and *hold it* | **Drag & Drop** (Acts like clicking and holding your mouse down to move windows or files). |
| **Fist / Idle** | ✊ Closed hand or natural resting pose | **Idle** (No action taken). |

---

## ⌨️ LEFT HAND — Hotkeys & System Tools
The left hand triggers keyboard shortcuts, volume, and takes screenshots.

*Note: For the best results, hold the pose clearly until you see the confirmation text pop up on the camera screen.*

| Hand Pose | What it looks like | Action Performed |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Index Up** | ☝️ Only index finger up | `Ctrl + Z` **(Undo)** |
| **Middle + Index** | ✌️ Peace sign | `Ctrl + C` **(Copy)** |
| **Ring Finger Up** | 💍 Only ring finger up | `Ctrl + V` **(Paste)** |
| **Pinky Up** | 🤙 Only pinky finger up | `Ctrl + S` **(Save)** |
| **Horns** | 🤘 Index and Pinky fingers up | `Alt + Tab` **(Switch Windows)** |
| **Open Palm** | 🖐️ All 5 fingers fully extended | **Takes a Screenshot** of your screen (Hold for 2 secs). |
| **Pinch Distance**| 🤏 Touch thumb/index, and slide apart | **Volume Control** (Wider gap = louder, closer gap = quieter) |

## 🎨 DRAWING CANVAS MODE
Want to draw notes right onto your webcam feed? You can toggle the canvas mode on and off using your physical keyboard. Once enabled, the canvas is controlled completely by your **Right Hand**.

#### How to use the Canvas:
1. Press the **`D` key** on your physical keyboard to toggle canvas mode ON.
2. Ensure only your **Right Index Finger (☝️)** is up.
3. Draw in the air! The line will follow your finger.

#### Canvas Gestures:
| Right Hand Pose | Action Performed |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Index Up (☝️)** | Draws ink where your finger points. |
| **Quick Fist (✊)** | "Lifts the pen" off the paper (stops drawing). |
| **Hold Fist (✊)** | Clear the entire whiteboard (Hold for 1.5 seconds). |

#### Canvas Keyboard Controls:
*You must be actively clicking/focused on the "Gesture Mouse" webcam window to use these physical keys.*
- **`C`** : Changes your drawing color (Cycles through Green, Blue, Red, Cyan, Yellow, White).
- **`S`** : Saves the current drawing as an image directly to your desktop folder.
- **`D`** : Exits drawing mode and returns to normal mouse mode.

## 🛑 APP CONTROLS & SYSTEM TRAY
You don't need to exit the program if you want to use a physical mouse for a minute. You can just Pause it.

| Keyboard Key | Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| **`P`** | **Pause / Resume** detection (Turns tracking off temporarily). |
| **`Q`** | **Quits** the application entirely. |

Alternatively, you can right-click the small **Green Hand Icon** in your Windows System Tray (bottom right corner of your screen near the clock) to:
* Quit the app 
* Pause the app 
* Toggle Drawing Canvas (without needing to focus the OpenCV window)
